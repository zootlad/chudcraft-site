About Chudcraft

Chudcraft is a custom Minecraft modpack featuring an extensive collection of mods designed to enhance your gameplay experience. From automation with Create to exploration with Biomes o' Plenty, Chudcraft offers something for every type of player.

Created by zootlad, this modpack brings together the best mods in the Minecraft community to create a unique and engaging experience. Whether you're building, exploring, or just clicking quota, Chudcraft has you covered.
